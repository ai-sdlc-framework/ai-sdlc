# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

This project adopts the **Contributor Covenant, version 2.1**.

The full text is available at: <https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/>

## Summary

We are committed to providing a welcoming and inclusive experience for everyone. We expect all participants to treat each other with respect and professionalism.

## Reporting

Instances of unacceptable behavior may be reported to the project maintainers. All complaints will be reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing standards of acceptable behavior and will take appropriate and fair corrective action in response to any behavior that they deem inappropriate.

This Code of Conduct is adapted from the [Contributor Covenant](https://www.contributor-covenant.org), version 2.1.
